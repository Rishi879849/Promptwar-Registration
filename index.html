What is Google PromptWars?

PromptWars is India's first end-to-end vibe coding competition. Use LLMs as your primary IDE, tackle real-world problems, and build at the speed of thought — just like your friendly neighborhood Spider-Dev.

⚡

Vibe Coding

State your requirements clearly, let LLMs write the code, and focus on system architecture, design, and user flow — like spinning a perfect web.

🧠

Gemini 2.5 API

Get direct sandbox keys and API credits to build with Google's most advanced multimodal models — your Gemini sense will tingle.

🕸️

Teams of 1–3

Register solo or assemble your super-squad of up to 3. Every Spider-Man needs their team — sometimes even the Avengers need help.

🎓

Google Mentorship

Stuck on an endpoint or prompt? Google Developer Experts (GDEs) and senior engineers will swing by to mentor you throughout the event.
